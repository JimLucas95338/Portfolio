üìñ User Stories

Epic 1: Risk Stratification & Patient Identification

Story 1.1: High-Risk Patient Identification

As a Primary Care Physician
I want to automatically identify patients at highest risk for expensive interventions
So that I can proactively manage their care and prevent costly events

Acceptance Criteria:
  • System identifies top 10% highest-risk patients in my panel
  • Risk scores are updated daily with new claims/clinical data
  • Predictions have >80% accuracy for identifying patients with >$10K annual costs
  • Interface shows risk factors contributing to each patient's score
  • System flags patients whose risk has increased significantly (>20 points)

Story 1.2: Care Gap Identification

As a Care Manager
I want to see all preventive care gaps for my patient population
So that I can prioritize outreach and improve quality metrics

Acceptance Criteria:
  • Dashboard shows gaps by HEDIS measure (Diabetes, Hypertension, etc.)
  • Patient lists are exportable for care team outreach
  • System tracks completion rates after interventions
  • Automated alerts for approaching deadlines

Epic 2: Quality Measures & Performance Tracking

Story 2.1: HEDIS Performance Monitoring

As an ACO Leader
I want to track our performance on all HEDIS quality measures in real-time
So that I can ensure we meet contract requirements and earn quality bonuses

Acceptance Criteria:
  • Real-time tracking of all contracted HEDIS measures
  • Benchmark comparison against national/regional averages
  • Trend analysis showing improvement over time
  • Provider-level performance breakdowns
  • Projection modeling for year-end performance

Epic 3: Financial Analytics & Shared Savings

Story 3.1: Shared Savings Tracking

As a Health System Executive
I want to monitor our progress toward shared savings targets
So that I can make strategic decisions to maximize financial performance

Acceptance Criteria:
  • Monthly PMPM cost tracking vs. benchmark
  • Shared savings projections based on current performance
  • Cost category breakdowns (inpatient, outpatient, pharmacy, etc.)
  • ROI calculations for intervention programs
  • Quality bonus impact modeling

Data Integration Requirements

  • Claims Data Integration: Real-time processing of medical, pharmacy, and dental claims
  • Clinical Data Sources: EHR integration via HL7 FHIR APIs
  • Quality Measure Data: HEDIS, CAHPS, and custom measure calculation
  • Provider Attribution: Panel management and attribution logic

Analytics & Machine Learning

  • Risk Stratification Models: Predictive algorithms for cost and utilization

  • Population Segmentation: Automated patient cohort identification

Performance Requirements

  • Data Latency: Claims processing within 24 hours
  • Dashboard Performance: Sub-2 second load times for all reports
  • Scalability: Support 100,000+ attributed lives
  • Availability: 99.9% uptime SLA
