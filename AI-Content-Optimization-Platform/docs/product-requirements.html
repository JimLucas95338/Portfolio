üéØ Problem Statement

Primary Problem

70% of content marketers struggle to create content that consistently engages their audience, leading to poor ROI on content marketing investments in a $600B+ global market.

Secondary Problems

  • Lack of data-driven optimization guidance - Creators rely on intuition rather than data
  • Platform-specific content requirements complexity - Each platform has different best practices
  • Limited competitive intelligence - No visibility into what works for competitors
  • Inefficient content iteration processes - Manual optimization takes hours per post
  • Poor performance prediction capabilities - No way to predict success before publishing

üöÄ Go-to-Market Strategy

Target Market Segments

Primary: SMB Marketing Teams

Small to medium businesses with 10-100 employees needing to scale content production efficiently.

  • Market Size: 32M SMBs globally
  • Pain Point: Limited marketing resources, need for efficiency
  • Value Prop: Professional-grade optimization at affordable price

Secondary: Marketing Agencies

Digital marketing agencies managing content for multiple clients simultaneously.

  • Market Size: 150K+ agencies worldwide
  • Pain Point: Scaling personalized content across clients
  • Value Prop: Multi-client management, white-label options

Pricing Strategy

  • Freemium: $0/month - 10 optimizations, basic analytics
  • Professional: $29/month - Unlimited optimizations, advanced analytics
  • Team: $99/month - Multi-user, collaboration features, API access
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing - White-label, custom integrations

Launch Strategy

Phase 1: Beta Launch (Month 1-2)

  • Invite 100 selected content creators and marketers
  • Focus on LinkedIn and Twitter optimization initially
  • Gather feedback and iterate based on real usage
  • Build case studies and testimonials

Phase 2: Public Launch (Month 3-4)

  • Launch freemium model with Product Hunt feature
  • Add Instagram and Facebook optimization
  • Content marketing campaign highlighting success stories
  • Influencer partnerships with marketing thought leaders

Success Criteria

  • Year 1: 10,000 active users, $500K ARR
  • Year 2: 50,000 active users, $3M ARR
  • Year 3: 150,000 active users, $12M ARR
