Core Product Features

  • AI Lead Scoring Engine
    Machine learning model that scores leads based on behavior, firmographics, and intent signals. Achieves 85% accuracy in predicting qualified leads with custom scoring models per customer.
  • Intelligent Chat Automation
    LLM-powered conversational AI that engages website visitors, responds within 2 seconds, maintains conversation context, and supports 50+ languages.
  • Predictive Analytics Dashboard
    Real-time insights into marketing performance with <1 second load time, trend analysis over 12-month periods, and automated insights generation.
  • CRM Integration Hub
    Seamless data synchronization with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and Microsoft Dynamics with <5 second latency and conflict resolution.
  • Personalization Engine
    Dynamic content delivery based on visitor profile and behavior within 200ms, supporting A/B testing and multi-variate testing.
